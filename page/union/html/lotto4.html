Hookbag Lucky Cash Sweepstakes Activity Rules

The Lucky Cash Sweepstakes activity is carried out once a week. Activity cycle is from 0 o'clock on Monday to 24 o'clock on Sunday.

Prize winners of each Period are drawn on the next Wednesday at 9 PM, following the conclusion of the activity.

Members participating in Lucky Cash Sweepstakes activities can win lucky Cash & Prize Entry Tickets by purchasing goods (Hookbag deduction time is just your lucky ticket issuing time) or completing site specified tasks. For example, any order over 50 Canadian dollars gives you 1 Cash & Prize Entry Ticket; order over 100 Canadian dollars and you get 2 Cash & Prize Entry Tickets; bind social networking site accounts such as Facebook account to Hookbag and you get 1 Cash & Prize Entry Ticket; users who have bound social networking site accounts such as Facebook account can get 1 Cash & Prize Entry Ticket every week if you share all kinds of Hookbag’s promotional information to your friends through your Facebook account or other social networking site accounts.

Winning numbers of Lucky Cash Sweepstakes activity are produced by Hookbag's random selection. Users can immediately collect your prize after winning in the Lucky Cash Sweepstakes activity.

The bonus will be sent to users’ cash account. Bonus in cash account can be used to pay for orders on Hookbag official website. Bonus in cash account can also be cashed via bank transfer, PayPal transfer, cashier's check or other channels and 10% of handling fees need to be charged for the cashing. Winning customer's award is valid for 7 days. If wining customers do not receive awards in time, and then it will be considered as an automatic waiver of the award. Hookbag will not assume any responsibility for the loss caused by any award expiration.

For a single ticket winning, the total amount of bonus possible is 125 Canadian dollars, including a 50 Canadian dollars cash prize and a 75 Canadian dollars task award. If a single member wins N (N>1) Cash & Prize Entry Tickets, the bonus amount is 50*N+75 Canadian dollars, in which there is a 50*N Canadian dollars cash prize and a 75 Canadian dollars task award. You can receive the 75 Canadian dollars task award after completing Hookbag’s specified tasks.

After receiving the cash award, you need to join Hookbag Commission Referral Program Activities to complete specified tasks and win the 75 Canadian dollars task award. Just input your member nickname of Hookbag Commission Referral Program Activities and agree with Hookbag’s Commission Referral Program Activities agreement after receiving the cash award, and then you can join Hookbag Commission Referral Program Activities.

After you join Hookbag Commission Referral Program Activities, the 75 Canadian dollars task award will appear in the Winners Details Page. There is a 25 Canadian dollars for Task 1. The task is for users to share the Lucky Cash Sweepstakes message to your Facebook, Twitter, Google or any other social networking site friends. There is another 50 Canadian dollars for Task 2. The task is for users to copy special links in task 2 to social networking sites, instant messaging tools, etc. or send the links to your friends by emails to invite 5 friends to register on Hookbag. For each friend registering through the special links, the user will get a 10 Canadian dollars bonus, and each friend will get a 5 Canadian dollars cash coupon, which can be used to deduct the goods amount purchased on Hookbag.

The C$50 task bonus is valid for 14 days after receiving the cash award. If wining customers do not complete the task in time, then it will be considered as an automatic waiver of the 75 Canadian dollars task bonus award. Hookbag will not assume any responsibility for any loss caused by task expiration.

In case that the Cash & Prize Entry Ticket generated by purchase wins a prize, then customers voluntarily give up the right of return, but the purchased goods are still under normal replacement and warranty. Hookbag assumes no responsibility for any loss caused by user's giving up the right of return.

If you have any questions in the Lucky Cash Sweepstakes activity, please contact website customer service. Receiving channels: email (serviceteam@hookbag.ca), customer service telephone (Toll Free: 877- 9876-Bag (877-9876-224)).
