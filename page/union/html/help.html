Brief Introduction

“ Lucky Cash “ - For every $50 that you spend, you will have a chance to win cash prizes. (The $50 Lucky Cash Ticket Qualification Amounts excludes: Taxes, Shipping and any (EHF) Environmental Handling Fees). Lucky Casg Winners will receive their winnings as well as extra Cash Bonuses if you share that you have won on your Facebook Account. We will make payments to: Credit Card/Debit Card Accounts or PayPal Accounts. Winning “Lucky Cash Sweepstakes" automatically enters Winners into our "Hookbag Commission Referral Program Club."


“ Hookbag Commission Referral Program” - How our program works. We ask that you share your information about your experience that you had with Hookbag on your Facebook Account. You will be given a Unique ID Link that you can share with your friends, co-workers and family. When they come to Hookbag, sign up and purchase an item(s) from Hookbag, and use the ID Link that you have provided them then you will make a commission on that sale. When your friends, co-workers or family sign up they will also get their own Unique ID Link to pass on to new referrals.(Important: Hookbag uses these ID Links to pay members their Direct Commissions and Commission Bonuses) The more people that sign up, the more money you will make, from any purchase/s that they make. There is no limit to how much you can earn or how many people are Linked to you or with you.

Direct Sales: A 3% Direct Sales Commission is automatically given to the closest Introducer/Upline of the person making the purchase

Indirect Sales:Indirect Commission is given to every Upline Member that is indirectly involved in the referral process that helped to creat a purhase on Hookbag. Commission Amounts will vary due to the number of people involved that eventually created a Purchase with Hookbag. These Commissions and Bonuses also apply to future purchases. Note: You must be an Active CRP Member to particpate and collect earnings.

FAQ

How can I win?

You can get a Lucky Cash & Prize Entry ticket by spending CAD 50 or more on Hookbag. The more tickets you get, the more chances you win.

What should I do to get all the bonuses after I have won?

You can get a CAD 50 Cash Prize by confirming Hookbag’s Winners Rules Agreement after you have won. You can get another CAD 50 task bonus by completing sharing tasks after you have won. After you complete the sharing tasks, you can get another CAD 25 task bonus by bringing in newly registered users. Meanwhile, each newly registered user that joins also earns a CAD 5 Gift Certificate per person just for joining.

How to get qualified for Hookbag’s Commission Referral Program Members?

Users who have won in Hookbag’s Lucky Cash Sweepstakes activities will become Hookbag’s Commission Referral Program Members. Other users can become Hookbag’s Commission Referral Program Members by filling in the introducer’s unique code during the initial registration process, and by also spending CAD 100 or more on Hookbag after their registration. Membership is valid for 1 year. If the cumulative spending reaches CAD 100 again within the validity, then the validity will extend.

How can I get a rebate?

For example, User A is a Hookbag Commission Referral Program Member. User A is User B’s direct introducer. When B makes a purchase on Hookbag, all Commission Referral Program members above B in the Program network will get different amounts of Commission Bonuses. User A will receive a 3% Direct Sales Commission from User B's total purchase. User C is User A’s direct introducer in the Commission Referral Program network and User C will receive a 1% of Passive Income from User B’s total purchase. User D is User C’s direct introducer in the Commission Referral Program network and User D will receive a 0.5% of Passive Income from User B’s total purchase. and so on. Each level will get half Commission amount of its lower level.
(Note: This purchase amount mentioned above does not include Shipping/Handling/Taxes.)

I am already a Hookbag’s Commission Referral Program Member, what methods do Hookbag provide to let me share and get Commission Bonuses?

Commission Referral Program members can share Hookbag’s website and bring registered users by sharing product images and links, Hookbag activities, Hookbag functions and by other methods. When brought registered users consume on Hookbag, you can get Commission Bonuses.

How to maintain my Hookbag Commission Referral Program Membership?

If your cumulative consumption reaches CAD 100 within the validity, your membership will extend for 1 year.

What should I do if I want to withdraw cash from my Cash Account?

Commission Referral Program members can freely withdraw cash from your Hookbag Cash Account. Available withdrawal channels are PayPal, credit card and company check.

Why can my Commission Bonuses only be cashed after 45 days?

Commission Bonuses will be transferred to Commission Bonuses winner’s Cash Account after the consumer completes the payment. If the consumer claims a return or refund within the 45 Days of Worry-Free Coverage, Commission Bonuses will be invalid. After the 45 Days of Worry-Free Coverage, Commission Bonuses will change from a hold on funds status to useable funds status. Under the useable funds status, Commission Bonuses can then be cashed.
