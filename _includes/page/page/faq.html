I am a reseller/government employee. How do I place an order with Hookbag.ca without being charged for tax?

All customers outside of British Columbia, Manitoba, Ontario, Quebec, and Saskatchewan will not be charged any tax. Therefore, customers in those locales do not need to fax or set up any reseller tax exemption form.

For resellers in British Columbia, Manitoba, Ontario, Quebec, and Saskatchewan:

Please place your first order as normal, and then email us at serviceteam@hookbag.ca with your reseller permit and this form, along with your Hookbag order number. We will then remove all tax from your order. Please to view and print Hookbag reseller tax exemption form.

For Government in British Columbia, Manitoba, Ontario, Quebec, and Saskatchewan:

Please place your first order as normal. After you've submitted your order, email us at serviceteam@hookbag.ca with your Tax Exempt ID and include the sales order number. If you have made several previous orders, please mention those sales order numbers and Hookbag.ca will refund your tax charge on those orders as well. If you have any questions, please give us a call at 877- 9876-Bag (877-9876-224).

Note: Sales to the Government of Canada are exempt from tax, only if the purchase is made with a federal government credit card.
