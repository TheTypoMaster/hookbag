General Return Policy

Hookbag.ca offers you hassle free returns for a full refund or replacement within 45 days from the delivery date (special products excluded).
Return for a refund or a replacement will not be accepted by Hookbag.ca beyond the 45 days (from the delivery date) of Worry- Free Coverage. You will have to mail the products directly to the manufacture for repair (if they provide such services) and assume the shipping costs generated during this process. Customers applying for return will receive our email notification and have two choices: to receive an equivalent replacement or to return for a full refund within 45 days of the delivery.

2.Qualification for Return(refund or exchange)
To qualify for a return (refund or exchange) all products require a valid Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) Number before being returned. Products must be 100% complete with original manufacturer’s packaging and standard Product Serial Number or UPC Code (originally placed and not tampered with). All packing materials, contents and documentation must be included. Kits and other items assembled after purchase must be disassembled and returned in the manufacturer's original packaging. All returns will be inspected and those non-qualified and / or non-conforming returns will be subject to a 15% restocking fee* at Hookbag.ca's sole discretion.

(Special / Custom / Heavy products are excluded, and cannot be returned).

3. Non-qualified Returns and Restocking Charges
Non-qualified and non-conforming returns are not included in Hookbag.ca return policy and, if returned, will be rejected or subject to a 15% restocking fee at Hookbag.ca's sole discretion. This includes (but not limited to) items that are wrongly ordered, items the client no longer wants after order is placed, or a product that the client ‘doesn’t like’ or is unsatisfied with. If the item is in proper working order and is NOT defective, the item will be considered non-qualifying and will be subject to rejection or a 15% restocking fee.

4. Defective, Wrong or Damaged Items
Hookbag.ca takes full responsibility for defective items, wrong items shipped or items damaged during delivery. Customers can replace the item online by filling out the RMA form for replacement.

5. Bundle Purchase
Items sold as part of a bundle special may not be returned or replaced individually for that individual item. If you wish to return or replace a Bundle Special, you must return the entire bundle.

6. Open Box Items
Hookbag.ca sells items that are listed as “open-box”. Such items are sold at greatly reduced prices and subject to a limited return policy. Open box items can only be returned for refund within 15 days of delivery. Hookbag.ca does not provide any replacement service for these items. As the items are sold at largely reduced price, the entire risk as to the quality and performance of these items is with the buyer. Although these items have been tested for functionality, they may have superficial physical defects including, but not limited to dings, dents or scratches. In case these items exhibit a functional defect after their purchase, the buyer (not the distributor, manufacturer, or Hookbag.ca) assumes the total cost of all necessary servicing or repairs, unless otherwise required by law. If you purchase a product at a largely reduced price, Hookbag.ca will only guarantee that you receive the product itself. Accessories may not be included with it. In that case, you need to buy the missing accessories by yourself.

Return Procedure

Simple steps for return:

  1. Visit our Online Returns Center and get a return mailing label and return mailing authorization. We will inform you within one business day whether your RMA is valid or not.
  2. Pack the items and return mailing authorization securely in the packing box used to deliver the item.
  3. Attach the return mailing label to the outside of the packing box (clear tape is best) and mark the RMA number on the outside of the packing box.
  4. Mail the package at your designated carrier's location. Returned goods will be processed within 7 business days (from the date Hookbag.ca receive your returns).
Note: Each return mailing label is uniquely assigned to a specific return. To make sure that you receive the correct refund, please do not pack items from other orders or other shipments in the same box.
