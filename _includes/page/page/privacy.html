Information Collection and Use Policy

Hookbag.ca highly honours our customer privacy. Hookbag.ca is the only owner of all information collected on this Website. We never distribute, sell, or rent personal information to any outside parties, except as outlined in this policy. Information collected includes your name, telephone number(s), e-mail address(es), shipping and billing addresses, and payment information such as your credit card number and its expiry date. We do require you to submit a user name and password of your choice for your future access to your account information. This is also used to help protect your privacy. We do not share this information. If you elect to receive our newsletter or special promotions, we will use your contact information to deliver these items.

Registration

To allow us to process your online orders, you must first complete the registration, during which you will be asked to provide your contact information such as your name, telephone number , e-mail and street addresses. This information is necessary for us to provide you services such as e-mail notification about your order status updates.

Order

Most of the information collected in the registration process will be used to process orders. For billing purposes and order fulfilment, we do ask that you to provide financial information such as your credit card number, the card's expiration date or money order, wire transfer or check information during the order process. If we have difficulty in processing an order, we will use the contact information you have provided to reach you and inform you of the issue(s). In order to process your credit card information, we must share your personal and financial information with your merchant bank for authorization and approval. This process is protected by an enhanced security system. Refer to the Security section below for more details on security measures and procedures. We do not share your personal and financial information with any third parties except those specified in the Sharing section of this policy.

Cookies

We customize certain web content based on your browser type and other information provided by our cookie. Should you opt to reject the cookie you can still browse our store but you cannot use the shopping cart to buy goods. The Hookbag.ca shopping cart cannot function without cookies being enabled so that the information necessary to handle your order is retained. If you disable cookies, Hookbag.ca will be unable to accept your online order. We will not share any personal information provided by this cookie with any third parties. However, we will link data kept in cookies to the personal information you submitted while on our site. This allows us to personalize your shopping experience. Some of our business partners (e.g. advertisers) use cookies on our site. We have no access to or control over these cookies. Therefore, this privacy policy does not cover the use of cookies by advertisers. It only covers the use of cookies by Hookbag.ca.

Log Files

We use IP addresses to administer the site, analyze trends, track user movement, and collect demographic information for analytical purposes only. We do not link IP addresses to personal information and we do not distribute or share IP information with any unrelated third parties (see "Sharing" below).

Sharing

We may share your information with third parties we employ to perform services on our behalf such as order fulfilment, credit processing, package delivery, postal and e-mail delivery, marketing assistance, data analysis and customer service. The third party companies we contract for these purposes have strictly limited access to your personal information and they may not use it for any other purpose. In such a situation, your personal information would only be transferred in a manner consistent with our pre-existing Privacy Policy. We must of course reserve the right to disclose your personally identifiable information as required by law or when we believe that disclosure is necessary or appropriate to protect our rights and/or comply with a court order, judicial proceeding or legal process served on our Website.

Links

Our website links to other websites, many of which have their own privacy policies. Hookbag.ca is not responsible for these privacy practices. Be sure to review the privacy policy on the site you're visiting. This Hookbag.ca privacy statement applies only to information collected on our website.

Minors

Hookbag.ca does not sell products for purchase by children. Youth-oriented products are offered for purchase by adults only. Those who are under the age of 18 may only use Hookbag.ca in the presence of a consenting parent or guardian. We are committed to protecting the welfare and privacy of children on the internet and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

Security

Hookbag.ca uses enhanced security measures to protect the confidentiality of our customers information. The sensitive information you submit via our website is protected both online and offline. When our registration/order form asks you to enter personal information such as your credit card number, that information is encrypted and fully protected with the best and most powerful encryption technology Secure Socket Layer(SSL). The Hookbag.ca website uses the most advanced form of SSL software available: 128-bit encryption by GeoTrust.Hookbag.ca provides limited access to each and all of our users information.  Only employees who need the information to perform a specific task (such as order processing) are granted access to personal information. Access to internet is restricted and protected by multiple firewalls and by complex password encryption. Access to our internal database is also strictly monitored and protected from outside access. All data backups and the servers on which we store personal information are kept in a high-level security room. Only personnel with proper security clearance have access to these restricted areas. Tape backups are not allowed to leave the premises without authorization. If you have any questions or concerns on Hookbag.ca’s security practices you can send us an email at
serviceteam@hookbag.ca.

Special Offers

Our special offers are sent only to customers who opt in to receive them. To respect customer privacy, Hookbag.ca offers you the option of not receiving these communications. At the time of your order you may choose not to receive special offers and related news by clicking the appropriate area. Furthermore, all special offers are sent by e-mail and include an opt-out link should you decide to no longer receive special offers and news from Hookbag.ca. Customers might still receive intermittent updates on products, services, specials or Hookbag.ca news. Please refer to the Choice/Opt-out section below for further details on how to be removed from our mailing list should you choose so..

Choice/Opt-out

For customers who no longer wish to receive special offers and related news from Hookbag.ca, we provide you several ways to do so. You may opt-out of receiving these communications by deselecting the "permission to send" field when processing your orders. You may also do it by sending a blank e-mail message to
serviceteam@hookbag.ca
with "unsubscribe" as the e-mail topic or using the link provided in all Hookbag.ca communications to unsubscribe automatically.

Correction/Updating/Reviewing Personal Information

Customers may review or change their stored account information such as e-mail or street address by visiting the Help section on our website. You will need your user name and password handy to access your account. If you forget both your user name and password, please contact our Customer Service Department for assistance.
